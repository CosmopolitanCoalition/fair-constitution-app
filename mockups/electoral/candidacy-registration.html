Candidacy registration

Any jurisdictionally associated resident can stand for any office in their jurisdictions — there are no other requirements. Registration stays open for the entire inter-election period: it opens the moment the prior election certifies and closes only at the finalist cutoff.

No requirements beyond jurisdictional residency — absolute right · Art. I (Right to Stand for Office)

What happens next

  1. The election board validates the registration — the only check is jurisdictional residency. Candidate validation · F-ELB-002 · available to R-08 Election Board Member · Art. II §2 (election integrity)
  2. Validated candidates enter the approval pool immediately and appear on the open ballot with live standings.
  3. At the finalist cutoff the top X per race lock onto the ranked ballot; everyone else stays write-in eligible. X = f(seats) · CLK-21 · as implemented (ledger #5)
