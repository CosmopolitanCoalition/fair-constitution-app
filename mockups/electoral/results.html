Single-winner variant — individual executive (RCV)

Single-winner ranked-choice voting applies only to the individual executive office model. The top 4 runners-up become the executive's advisors and alternates automatically — derived by sequential exclusion: re-run the count without the winner, then without the top two, and so on, yielding the ranked top five.
