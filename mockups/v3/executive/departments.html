About this surface

What this screen does: the registry of departments — create one by legislative act, follow each board's appointment pipeline, and open a department for its roster, nominations, removals, and reporting. Consent votes happen in the session console.

How a department moves: from chartered, through board nomination and consent, to operating and reporting — with removal votes and re-chartering (or dissolution) along the way. Governors are politically neutral with default 10-year civil-officer terms.
