Your service is protected

A guaranteed protection — it can't be turned off
  • No interference. Your employer cannot penalize, dismiss, or obstruct you for serving; nobody — public or private — may impede a civic obligation. No one may interfere with a civic obligation.
  • No fees, ever. No payment, fee, or fine can be required of you to exercise a civic right or fulfill this obligation — attendance, filings, and verification cost you nothing. No payment can ever be required to exercise a civic right.

These two protections are on the constitution's list of actions forbidden to legislatures — they shield jurors from both interference and charges.
