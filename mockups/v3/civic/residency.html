About this surface

This screen walks you through establishing residency: declare where you live, let your location pattern build up over time, then confirm. Moving later re-runs the same steps, and population records update automatically.

How your claim progresses: Declared → Ping-Monitoring → Threshold-Met → Verified → Active. Along the way you become a resident of every jurisdiction that contains your home (formally: jurisdictional association).

The boundary map on this page is a stylized stand-in — production uses Leaflet + PostGIS (point-in-polygon against the county boundary from geoBoundaries).

Your right to reside is guaranteed, and authority rests on your consent.
