Register as an advocate

Advocates represent people in court. Everyone has the right to competent legal representation — registering here makes you eligible to provide it within a jurisdiction, under qualifications that jurisdiction's law defines.

Everyone has the right to a zealous, competent advocate.

What registration unlocks

  • Filing civil and criminal cases on behalf of clients.
  • Motions, evidence submission, and briefs in the advocate console.
  • Appearing in any court of the registering jurisdiction, including constitutional challenges.

Representing yourself never requires registration — this role exists so others can be competently represented. Everyone has the right to a fair trial and to legal representation.
