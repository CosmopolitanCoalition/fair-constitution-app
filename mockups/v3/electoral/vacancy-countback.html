The re-run

Re-run source: the prior 2030 New York County election — 287,540 valid ballots, 9 seats, Droop quota floor(287,540 ÷ 10) + 1 = 28,755. The engine strikes the vacated member and continues the count from the voters' next preferences. The re-run is universal — every prior ballot counts, with no faction filtering anywhere in the procedure.

Gold tick = the Droop quota. A continuing candidate who reaches it fills the seat; ballots with no remaining preference become exhausted.
