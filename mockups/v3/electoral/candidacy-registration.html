What happens next

  1. The election board validates the registration — the only check is jurisdictional residency. Candidate validation, done by an election board member.
  2. Validated candidates enter the approval pool immediately and appear on the open ballot with live standings.
  3. When the cutoff arrives, the top candidates lock onto the ranked ballot — 3 for each seat, so 27 in this race — and everyone else stays write-in eligible. How many lock in depends on the number of seats (ledger #5).

Your public record — votes attended, statements, civic actions — attaches to the candidacy automatically.
