Instant multi-winner PR-STV with the Droop quota: every candidate reaching the quota is elected, surpluses transfer at fractional value (Gregory method), and all 5 seats fill in one count. Droop quota — the smallest vote total that only 5 candidates can all reach: floor(votes ÷ (seats + 1)) + 1. Proportional ranked-choice counting with the Droop quota — a permanent rule
