Federation — peers, sync, and authority

Every jurisdiction's records have exactly one authoritative instance; every other instance mirrors them. Peers discover each other, establish trust, and exchange records under full faith and credit — when copies diverge, the authoritative instance wins, and the resolution is audit-chained.

Authority claims

Authoritative-instance migration

When a jurisdiction stands up its own server it becomes the authoritative copy for its records — for example, Kings County standing up brooklyn.cga.example and spinning out of albany-commons.example. Three steps, reversible until the flip.

    Border settlement

    Boundary changes between jurisdictions pass by a supermajority of the affected population — the people inside the moving boundary decide, not the legislatures around them.
