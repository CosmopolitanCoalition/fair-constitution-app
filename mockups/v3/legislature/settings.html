About this surface

This screen lists the settings a legislature can change by passing a bill. A range check wraps the ordinary bill flow so out-of-range values are rejected before any vote. Rules of order and the ethics code are adopted the same way. The protected bounds themselves can only be changed by a full constitutional amendment.

A setting change is a bill whose enactment records the new value, linked to the act with its effective date.
