About this surface

What this screen does: the legislature delegates a question to a population-wide vote, the question queues for the next jurisdiction-wide ballot, and passed acts can later be modified or repealed — except population-supermajority acts, which are shielded for the rest of the term. Citizen petitions reach the same ballot too.

Life of a referendum question: Delegated/Queued → Scheduled → Voted → Passed (matching threshold) | Failed → Law → [supermajority-shielded same term] → ordinary law after the general election.
