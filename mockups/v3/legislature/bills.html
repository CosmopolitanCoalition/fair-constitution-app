    About this surface

    What this screen does: introduce a bill and watch it move through the lifecycle — from introduction, to committee, to a floor vote, to enactment and publication. One-chamber and two-chamber legislatures both start here; two-chamber bills need both kinds of seats to agree, which you can follow on the bill detail page. Bills that change an amendable setting get a range check before the vote.

    The bill lifecycle: shown in the legend above. The Curfew Ordinance row shows the Challenged stage, where a court can review and edit an enacted law.
