    About this surface

    What this screen does: it is the procedural side of a regular session — the meeting itself happens live in the chamber room. From here the Speaker opens the meeting, takes attendance, confirms the quorum, then works the agenda in order. Motions flow through their own handling steps, and a separate path covers what happens if the quorum fails. The session-due banner comes from the 90-day meeting deadline.

    Motion lifecycle: submitted → seconded / recognized → debated → voted → adopted or failed; an active emergency power drives agenda item 1 when present.
