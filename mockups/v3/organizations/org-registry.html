About this surface

What this screen does: registering an organization begins here; the registry then links onward to each organization's profile for endorsement, membership and worker joining, and to the worker-board-seats scale.

Under the hood: there is no faction registration anywhere in the application — parties are ordinary organizations, and the endorsement records (from any organization or individual to any candidate) are what feed the proportionality math. As implemented (ledger #1)

Organization lifecycle: registered → active → endorsing → worker board seats → transfer pending → transferred → converted public ↔ private → dissolved.
