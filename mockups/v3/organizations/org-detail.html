Endorsements

Two forms meet here: a candidate files an endorsement request Endorsement request (available to a candidate · Freedom of assembly), and the organization answers with a grant Candidate endorsement grant (available to the organization's agent · creates the endorsement record · Freedom of assembly). Granting makes the candidate an endorsed candidate.

Endorsement linkage feeds proportionality. There is no faction layer — committee assignment and observer proportionality read the polymorphic endorsement graph instead. Individuals endorse the same way organizations do. As implemented (ledger #1)
