World of Statecraft — the game layer

Governance as a live, social activity — the human acts in the foreground, the bureaucracy automated behind them.

This is the player's companion to the v1 operations mockups. v1 maps every role, workflow, and form for the build team. v2 maps the same system the way the people in it experience it: an election night, a committee hearing, founding an org, a court case, a market — each a guided journey, each passing through a Live Civic Room where people actually speak, debate, persuade, and vote.

A mockup of the game layer, not the running application. Everything is simulated in-page. v2 reuses v1's design system and links into v1 for the formal steps; the economy and reach surfaces are design-ahead and marked Planned. Use the controls below to switch persona, jurisdiction, and game scenarios.
