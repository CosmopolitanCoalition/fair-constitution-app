Cosmopolitan Governance App — design mockups

Representation for Every One, Every Where, All the Time.

A navigable design contract for the operational app: every role, every workflow, every form — over real Earth geography with fictional people. Built from A Fair Constitution (the Cosmopolitan Template).

This is a mockup site, not the running application. Interactions are simulated; no data leaves the page. Use the controls in the bar below to assume any of the 30 constitutional roles, switch jurisdictions, flip scenarios, and test locales. Sections still to be built are marked with their build stage. The application itself is free software, released to the public under open licenses.

Enter as a role

All 30 constitutional roles in their 7 tiers (roles chart Sheet 1). Each card opens that role’s entry surface with the demo persona preset. Voting and candidacy require nothing beyond jurisdictional residency · Art. I

Workflow directory

All 80 workflows from the catalog inventory (CIV 8 · ELE 10 · LEG 20 · EXE 9 · JUD 9 · ORG 10 · JUR 9 · SYS 5). Each gets a step-by-step walkthrough page with every BRANCH and failure path.

The demo world

Geography — real, pre-united

The app preloads actual Earth geography — planet to neighborhood, nearly a million jurisdictions, with depth varying honestly by country (the United States chain ends at the county level). Nations parent directly to Earth — there is no supranational tier.

People & organizations — fictional

Twelve named personas (plus stand-ins so every role is assumable) and endorsing organizations of every organization_type. There is no faction layer — any organization or individual can endorse any candidate.
